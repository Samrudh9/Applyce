Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and net banking through our secure payment gateway.
Is there a free trial?
Our free plan lets you try all basic features with 3 scans per day. No credit card required to get started!
What counts as a resume scan?
Each time you upload a resume for analysis, it counts as one scan. Re-analyzing the same resume counts as a new scan.
