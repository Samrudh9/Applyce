What is SkillFit?

SkillFit is an intelligent, self-learning career recommendation platform that analyzes your resume, predicts optimal career paths, evaluates ATS compatibility, and continuously improves through user feedback.

Unlike traditional career tools, SkillFit uses a Bayesian learning algorithm that gets smarter with every user interaction. The more feedback we receive, the more accurate our predictions become.

Self-Learning Engine

Our self-learning engine is what makes SkillFit unique. Here's how it works:

1. Pattern Recognition: We track skill-to-career associations from every resume analyzed.
2. Feedback Loop: When you give thumbs up üëç or thumbs down üëé, we adjust our confidence scores.
3. Bayesian Update: More feedback = higher certainty. The formula: confidence = (positive + 1) / (positive + negative + 2)
4. Continuous Improvement: Every interaction makes SkillFit smarter for the next user.
